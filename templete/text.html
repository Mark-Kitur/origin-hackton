Patient Recommendations: Diabetes & Hypertension

Diabetes Recommendations

High Blood Sugar

Meal Recommended Foods
Breakfast Uji (millet porridge) with a small amount of honey and ground nuts.
Lunch Grilled Tilapia with kachumbari (tomato and onion salad) and sukuma wiki.
Supper Stewed Lentils with cabbage and whole grain chapati.

Exercise Recommendations

  • 30-60 minutes of light physical activity (walking, cycling) daily.
  • Avoid high-intensity workouts if blood sugar is very high.

Normal Blood Sugar

Meal Recommended Foods
Breakfast Whole Grain Bread with avocado and a boiled egg.
Lunch Mixed Vegetable Curry served with brown rice.
Supper Grilled Chicken with ugali and spinach.

Exercise Recommendations

  • 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity weekly.
  • Strength training exercises twice a week.

Low Blood Sugar

Meal Recommended Foods
Breakfast Pancakes made with whole wheat flour topped with banana and nuts.
Lunch Beef Stew with potatoes and carrots, served with rice.
Supper Samosas (vegetable or meat) with fresh fruit.

Exercise Recommendations

  • Avoid intense workouts when blood sugar is low.
  • Always carry fast-acting carbohydrates (like glucose tablets) during exercise.

Hypertension Recommendations

High Blood Pressure

Meal Recommended Foods
Breakfast Oatmeal topped with fresh fruit (like bananas or mango).
Lunch Grilled Chicken Salad with mixed vegetables and a light vinaigrette dressing.
Supper Vegetable Stir-fry with tofu, served over brown rice.

Exercise Recommendations

  • 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity (walking, swimming) each week.
  • Incorporate yoga or meditation to manage stress.

Meal Recommended Foods
